FAQs

How do you make your mattresses flame retardant?
We utilize natural flame-resistant materials, such as organic wool, which inherently meets flammability standards without the need for chemical additives. This approach ensures safety while maintaining an eco-friendly and non-toxic sleep environment.
What about side-to-side bounce?
Our mattresses are designed with materials like natural latex and individually wrapped coils to minimize motion transfer. This construction ensures that movement on one side of the bed doesn't disturb the other, providing a restful sleep experience.
Source: Eco Terra Beds
How do I know which mattress is suitable for me?
Selecting the right mattress depends on your sleeping position, body type, and personal comfort preferences. For instance, side sleepers may benefit from a softer mattress that cushions pressure points, while back sleepers might prefer a firmer surface for spinal alignment. Consulting with our sleep specialists can help you find the perfect match for your needs.
Source: Naturepedic
What about allergies to latex or wool?
While natural latex and wool are generally hypoallergenic, individuals with specific sensitivities should exercise caution. Our latex mattresses are processed to remove most allergenic proteins, reducing the risk of reactions. However, if you have known allergies, it's advisable to consult with a healthcare professional before making a purchase.
Source: Reddit, Brooklyn Bedding
Will this fix my back/shoulder/hip pain?
Our mattresses are designed to provide optimal support and pressure relief, which can alleviate discomfort in the back, shoulders, and hips. However, individual results may vary, and it's important to consider other factors such as posture and overall health. We recommend trying the mattress and consulting with a healthcare provider for persistent pain issues.
Are my new quilted mattress and pad covers machine washable?
Yes, our quilted mattress and pad covers are designed for easy maintenance and are machine washable. Always follow the care instructions provided to ensure longevity and performance.
Will I sleep hotter on this mattress?
Our mattresses incorporate breathable materials like natural latex and organic cotton to promote airflow and regulate temperature. This design helps dissipate heat and keeps you cool throughout the night.
Can I get a custom size?
Absolutely! We offer custom-sized mattresses to fit unique bed frames or specific space requirements. Contact us with your dimensions, and we'll craft a mattress tailored to your needs.
What are the mattress and foundation size standards?
Standard mattress sizes include Twin (38" x 75"), Full (54" x 75"), Queen (60" x 80"), King (76" x 80"), and California King (72" x 84"). Foundations are designed to match these dimensions, providing proper support and stability.
Why does SC41 use Talalay Latex in your mattresses?
We choose Talalay Latex for its superior comfort, durability, and breathability. This material offers consistent support and pressure relief, enhancing sleep quality. Additionally, Talalay Latex is hypoallergenic and environmentally friendly, aligning with our commitment to sustainable practices.
How do I know when I'm getting a chemically safe mattress?
Look for certifications such as GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard), GOLS (Global Organic Latex Standard), and OEKO-TEX. These labels ensure that the mattress meets strict safety and environmental standards, free from harmful chemicals and toxins.
Source: Architectural Digest, GQ
Why are your mattress coils wrapped in polypropylene?
Wrapping coils in polypropylene fabric helps isolate motion and reduces noise, enhancing sleep quality. This material is durable, lightweight, and resistant to moisture, contributing to the overall longevity and performance of the mattress.
